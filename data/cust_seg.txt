--- Balanced Value Shoppers ---
Description: Customers who seek good value and are activated by introductory offers and flexible bundles.
Promotions:
- Entry-price packs
- First-purchase discounts online
- Entry-price bundles
- Mix & match packs
- Threshold free shipping (e.g., 'Free delivery over $50')

--- Digital Wine Enthusiasts ---
Description: Customers with high web engagement who respond to targeted online offers and subscriptions.
Promotions:
- Targeted web coupons
- Bundles
- Time-limited promos
- Web-only drops
- Loyalty multipliers
- Subscriptions/auto-ship for wine

--- Price-Conscious Wine Buyers ---
Description: Price-sensitive customers with low web engagement who respond to multi-bottle savings and free delivery.
Promotions:
- Value multi-bottle packs
- Buy more, save more offers
- First-purchase discount
- Free delivery windows
